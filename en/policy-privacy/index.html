Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · This policy applies to meixner-tobias.com

Protecting personal data is important to me. This policy explains which data is collected on this website, for what purpose this happens and which rights you have.

1 Controller

Tobias Meixner
c/o Online-Impressum #7711
Europaring 90 · 53757 Sankt Augustin · Germany
Email: contact@meixner-tobias.com

2 Hosting

No consent required
GitHub Pages
This website is hosted via GitHub Pages. Provider: GitHub, Inc., 88 Colin P. Kelly Jr. Street, San Francisco, CA 94107, USA. When the website is accessed, connection data is automatically processed by GitHub. Transfer to the USA takes place on the basis of standard contractual clauses (Art. 46 GDPR).
GitHub privacy policy: docs.github.com

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in the technical provision of the website).

3 Server log files

When you access the website, the hosting provider automatically records the following data in server log files: IP address, date and time, accessed page, browser type and version, operating system and referrer URL.

This data is not merged with other data sources. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR. The legitimate interest lies in the security and stability of the website.

4 Cookies & consent management

This website uses cookies and similar technologies where this is technically necessary for operation or where you have given consent.

Categories

Technically necessary
Necessary cookies
Cookies required for the basic operation of the website. No consent required. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR.
Consent required
Analytics & marketing cookies
Cookies from Google Analytics 4 and Google Ads for measurement and optimization. These are only set after active consent. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR.

Consent Management (CookieScript)

CookieScript is used to manage consent. CookieScript stores your consent decision in the browser and automatically handles Google Consent Mode v2.

You can withdraw your consent at any time by clicking the "Cookie settings" link in the footer.

Legal basis for storing your consent decision: Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR in conjunction with Art. 7 GDPR (obligation to prove consent).

5 Contact form (Formspree)

Contract basis
Formspree, Inc.
Formspree Inc., 2261 Market Street #4990, San Francisco, CA 94114, USA.
Privacy policy: formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy

If you use the contact form, the entered data (name, email, optional company, subject and message) is transmitted to Formspree and forwarded from there by email. Formspree processes the data on servers in the USA on the basis of standard contractual clauses (Art. 46 GDPR). A data processing agreement (DPA) exists with Formspree.

The transmitted data is used exclusively to process your request and is deleted after processing is complete, unless statutory retention obligations apply.

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (pre-contractual measures) or Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR.

6 Google Tag Manager

Technically necessary
Google Tag Manager
Google Ireland Limited, Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4, Ireland.
Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

GTM is used for central management of JavaScript tags. GTM itself sets no cookies and does not process personal data. It becomes relevant for data protection only through the services loaded through it (see the respective sections).

GTM respects Consent Mode v2: tracking tags are only activated when consent has been granted.

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in technically efficient tag management).

7 Google Analytics 4 (GA4)

Consent required
Google Analytics 4
Google Ireland Limited, Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4, Ireland.
Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

GA4 uses cookies and similar technologies to collect, among other things, the following data:

  • Page views, time on page and bounce rate
  • Device information (browser type, operating system, screen resolution)
  • Approximate location (country/region, no exact IP address)
  • Referrer information (where visitors come from)
  • Conversion events (for example form submissions)

The IP address is anonymized before transmission. GA4 is fully activated only after active consent (Consent Mode v2). A data processing agreement exists between me and Google (Art. 28 GDPR). Data may be transferred to the USA (see section 12).

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent).

Further information: support.google.com/analytics

8 Google Ads & conversion tracking

Consent required
Google Ads / Google Ads Conversion Tracking
Google Ireland Limited, Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4, Ireland.
Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Google Ads conversion tracking sets a conversion cookie after defined actions (for example form submission). The cookie is valid for 30 days and does not contain personal data.

In addition, Enhanced Conversions is used: data provided by the user (for example email) is anonymized using SHA-256 hashing before transmission.

Conversion tracking is activated only after consent (Consent Mode v2 implemented). Data may be transferred to the USA (see section 12).

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent).

9 Google Search Console

No consent required
Google Search Console
Google Ireland Limited, Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4, Ireland.
Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Search Console is a webmaster tool and does not set cookies on this website. It evaluates aggregated search data from which no conclusions about individual persons are possible.

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in search engine optimization).

10 Google Fonts

Technically necessary
Google Fonts (via Google Fonts API)
Google Ireland Limited, Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4, Ireland.

This website loads fonts via the Google Fonts API. Your IP address is transmitted to Google servers. According to the current assessment, Google Fonts does not set cookies for identification.

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR. Data may be transferred to the USA (see section 12).

11 Gumroad (digital products & payment processing)

Consent required
Gumroad, Inc.
Gumroad, Inc., 548 Market St #72410, San Francisco, CA 94104, USA.
Privacy policy: gumroad.com/privacy

Digital products are sold and delivered via the subpage shop.meixner-tobias.com and via links to tobiasmeixner.gumroad.com. Checkout and payment processing are carried out entirely through Gumroad, Inc. Gumroad acts as Merchant of Record and is responsible for processing including sales tax and VAT compliance.

Gumroad is certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework. In addition, standard contractual clauses (SCC) pursuant to Art. 46(2)(c) GDPR apply.

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of a contract). Marketing cookies from Gumroad are based on Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent), where such cookies are set.

12 Transfers to the USA

Various services used on this website, especially Google services and Formspree, transfer data to the USA. Since the EU-US Data Privacy Framework (DPF), valid since July 2023, the USA is considered a safe third country for certified companies.

Google LLC and GitHub Inc. are certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework. In addition, standard contractual clauses (SCC) pursuant to Art. 46(2)(c) GDPR are in place as further safeguards.

13 Your rights as a data subject

You have the following rights with respect to the personal data concerning you:

  • Access (Art. 15 GDPR) - you may request information about your data stored by me.
  • Rectification (Art. 16 GDPR) - you may request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure (Art. 17 GDPR) - you may request deletion of your data unless statutory retention obligations apply.
  • Restriction (Art. 18 GDPR) - you may request restriction of processing.
  • Data portability (Art. 20 GDPR) - you may request your data in a commonly used format.
  • Objection (Art. 21 GDPR) - you may object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Complaint - you have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority, in particular with the Bavarian Data Protection Authority (BayLDA), Promenade 18, 91522 Ansbach.

To exercise your rights, please contact: contact@meixner-tobias.com

14 Withdrawal of consent

Consent that has been granted can be withdrawn at any time with effect for the future. This applies in particular to consent for analytics and marketing cookies.

Cookie consent can be adjusted or withdrawn at any time via the "Cookie settings" link in the footer.

For withdrawal of other consent, an informal email to contact@meixner-tobias.com is sufficient.

15 Changes to this policy

This privacy policy will be updated if the website or the services used change, or if legal requirements change. The current version is indicated in the page header.
